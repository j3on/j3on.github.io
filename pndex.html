About Me.

Greetings, I'm Jeon (pronounced 'Jayon'), or 'Jay' for short. I'm a self taught front-end web developer based in southern California.

What drawn me to web development was the challenge it brings to not only your ability to solve problems, but also your ability to adapt to change. I understand that this field is constantly evolving and requires you to be constantly learning which excites me. The satisfaction of producing a site from scratch and seeing it come to life is an awesome feeling as well.

I love the design aspect of the web dev field and I really enjoy working with other creative and innovative people. I look forward to diving even deeper into this field while learning more frameworks and languages to further enhance my skillset.

When I'm not coding, you can find me in the gym lifting heavy things, bumping my head to the newest hip-hop album, or grabbing a drink at a bar with some friends.
